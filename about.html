How To Get To Robson Street


How To Get To Robson Street


By Bus:


There is a direct #5 bus service going to Robson Street/Downtown.

By Car:


There are ample metered parking spots along Robson Street between Burrard and Jervis, along with numerous paid parking lots in the surrounding area.

By Skytrain:


Robson Street is located three blocks South of Burrard Skytrain Station.

By Canada Line:


Robson Street is located three blocks West and one block South of the City Centre Canada Line Station.

On Foot:


Robson Street is a short walk from any point of the downtown core. Surrounding neighborhoods include Gastown, West End and Coal Harbour.
